About Me

I am a Front End Web Developer with hands-on experience in designing, developing, testing and deploying responsive websites using HTML/CSS/Javascript and many different frontend frameworks. I also have technical and data support experience in industries ranging from logistics, software development to dental insurance. Both the knowledge and the experience tell me that the quality of the user interaction with the products and services is the key factor of success.

I have been living in San Francisco Bay Area for over 20 years. San Francisco is a very beautiful city with the most comfortable weather. Because of this unique weather, we can always enjoy the world-famous sourdough bread. I originally came from Hong Kong. Hong Kong is the Pearl of the Orient, and it is such an energetic city that there are tons of delicous food and shopping opportunities 24/7.

I am a married woman. My husband and I have two beautiful kids and they are the love of our lives. In my leisure, I enjoy reading, swimming, hiking, and traveling with my family.
